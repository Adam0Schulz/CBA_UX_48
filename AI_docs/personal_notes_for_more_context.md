### Persona Going Through This Journey

**Brian B√¶k, 38** ‚Äì DIY weekend renovator. Values **planning, clarity, and efficiency**, but struggles with **uncertain availability, slow rental process, and unclear safety info**.

### Expectations (before starting)

- ‚ÄúThe process should be quick ‚Äî I just want to get my tool and start my project.‚Äù
- ‚ÄúThey‚Äôll help me if I‚Äôm unsure about specs or usage.‚Äù

### Stages & Actions

**1. Planning the Project**

- 1.1 Identifies the tools needed for his weekend DIY project.
- 1.2 Checks FOG‚Äôs website or calls the store for availability (information is unclear). **(Emotional rating: 2 ‚Äî frustrated by uncertainty)**

**2. Visiting the Store**

- 2.1 Drives to the store, expecting to pick up the tool.

- 2.3 Learns the tool is unavailable ‚Üí must go to another store or delay project. **(Emotional rating: 1 ‚Äî very frustrated, wasted time)**

- 4.1 Takes the tool home.

## UX sustainability canvas

### üå± Sustainable UX Canvas ‚Äì FOG Equipment Rental App

#### **E ‚Äì Environmental Sustainability**

**Visible Design (UI)**

- Dark mode by default ‚Üí reduces energy use on OLED devices.
- Lightweight visuals ‚Üí use images over video where possible; avoid autoplay.
- Clear CO‚ÇÇ impact nudges ‚Üí ‚ÄúRenting saves X kg CO‚ÇÇ compared to buying.‚Äù
- Show sustainability tips in context (e.g., ‚ÄúReturn on time = reduces need for extra production‚Äù).

**Invisible Design (Technical)**

- Green hosting provider for app infrastructure.
- Minified & optimized code to reduce load and energy waste.
- Lazy loading for photos/videos of tools.
- Caching & local storage for frequently viewed items (catalogue).
- Optimized image formats (WebP, SVG).

---

#### **S ‚Äì Social Sustainability**

**Accessibility & Inclusion**

- WCAG-compliant: high contrast, alt text, semantic HTML.
- Multi-language support for Denmark‚Äôs diverse population.
- Works on older/low-performance devices (Brian might be on budget phones).

**Ethical UX & Behavior**

- Avoid dark patterns in booking (no hidden fees).
- Clear, upfront pricing and rental terms.
- Responsible nudges ‚Üí return reminders (no guilt, just helpful).

**Equity & Representation**

- Gender-neutral language in onboarding/profile.
- Diverse imagery in promotional visuals (not just ‚Äúhandy men‚Äù).
- Consider inclusive instructions (visual + text + video guides).

**Digital Balance**

- Limit notifications to essentials (reminders, confirmations).
- No infinite scrolling catalogue ‚Äî structured browsing instead.

---

#### **G ‚Äì Governance (Ethics & Strategy)**

- **Strategic Anchoring:** Sustainability (tool sharing > buying) positioned as a core business value.
- **Ethical Guidelines:** Internal design principle ‚Üí ‚ÄúNo hidden costs, no manipulative upselling.‚Äù
- **Authority for Designers:** UX team empowered to choose greener defaults (dark mode, caching, sustainable nudges).
- **Measurement:** Regularly test app with Ecograder / Website Carbon to monitor footprint.
- **Transparency:** Communicate sustainability metrics to users (paperless receipts, CO‚ÇÇ savings, shared tool lifespan).
